Galacticaa Widget 2.0

∴ August 10, 2005 at 1:20 pm | Site News

The new version of the Galacticaa Dashboard widget is now available!

We originally planned to release this widget at the very start of Season 2, but it took a bit longer to develop than we had expected. But those of you who downloaded our original Season 2 Countdown widget should be very pleased.

Here’s a quick list of features:

  • Get the scoop on the next episode of Battlestar Galactica!
  • Get episode name, summary, photo, and airdate for each new episode
  • Display time remaining until the next episode of Galactica starts (optional)
  • Customize episode start time to match your preferred viewing time
  • Find out if the next episode is new or a repeat
  • Relish in the BSG-inspired interface, including cut corners!
  • …and a few surprises!

Special thanks to Ted O’Connor, Chris Radcliff, Danny Markham, and David Bills for extensively beta-testing this widget.
