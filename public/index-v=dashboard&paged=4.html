BSG Season 2 DVD hits stores!

∴ December 20, 2005 at 10:55 am | Merchandise

With just a few days left to go before Christmas, Universal has released the first half of Season 2 on DVD.

The disc contains the following features:

• 3 discs
• 10 episodes
• 440 minutes
• Deleted scenes
• Episode commentaries (from the podcasts)
• Sneak peek at the 2nd half of Season 2
• Dolby Digital 5.1 sound
• Widescreen anamorphic presentation

Notably, though, the DVD does not contain the extended, 90-minute version of the “Pegasus” episode where Galactica meets up with another Battlestar which survived the Cylon attack. Current plans call for this episode to be included in the DVD for the 2nd half of DVD… which will probably appear sometime this summer.

Lastly, I love the fact that Amazon already has 67 reviews for this DVD. That’s a passionate fanbase for you!

UPDATE: During today’s marathon, the SciFi channel is promoting the new DVD with commercials that mention a special DVD-only website. Not much new there, but there’s a high quality version of the DVD trailer that appeared on the web last week.

Frak! No extended “Pegasus” episode on Season 2 DVD!

∴ December 16, 2005 at 7:56 pm | Battlestar News, Merchandise

According to a report from TV Shows on DVD, the Season 2 DVD will NOT contain the extended cut of the “Pegasus” episode.

Fans of Battlestar Galactica were excited when Ron Moore talked about an extended episode of “Pegasus,” the half-season finale, during his PodCast for the show. Moore said that the extended episode would be released on DVD, and it was assumed that it would be on the 2.0 set. Reviewers, and some fans, have already obtained the 2.0 set (which streets on Tuesday, 12/20), but the extended episode wasn’t included. Message boards are filled with fuming fans, but we have good news that should extinguish the flames.

I spoke with a contact at Universal yesterday and asked about the extended episode. I was told that they couldn’t ready the episode in time for the set, but it would be the first episode in the next set (the second half of season 2). This sounds like a great idea since fans will own both versions of the episode, unlike the SG-1 extended episode issue. MGM released the shorter cut of “Threads” in the season 8 set, and fans wishing to obtain the longer version must send in their current disc, or a proof of purchase for the set. It’s more complicated than Universal’s solution.

This is definitely disappointing. While I’m glad there will still be an opportunity to view the extended cut, it sucks we now have to wait another 6 months or so to see it. (Though, on the other hand, when the 2nd Season 2 DVD does show up, it will be a much-needed antidote to Galactica withdrawl syndrome as we wait for Season 3 to begin.)

(Thanks to Justine at All about Tahmoh Penikett for bringing this sad news to our attention.)

Galactica goes mobile with SMS alerts and ringtones

∴ December 15, 2005 at 9:33 am | Merchandise

Galactica fans can now signup for free mobile alerts from the SciFi Channel. While the description of this feature is rather bare (”Get the latest SCI FI text updates on your cell phone.”), presumably it will send alerts when new episodes or other BSG-related content air on SciFi.

After entering your cellphone number and network, you can choose to receive alerts for Battlestar Galactica, Stargate Atlantis, Stargate SG-1, or Ghost Hunters.
